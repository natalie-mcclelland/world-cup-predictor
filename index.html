How to Play

  • Step 1: Fill out your Group Stage predictions.
  • Step 2: Enter your Bonus picks and Name in the "Bonus & Export" tab.
  • Final Step: Click "Generate My Code", copy the result, and send it to your organizer.
  • REMEMBER: You need to come back at each knockout phase to update your knockout predictions, regenerate your code and send on to the organizer.

    • When predicting the knockout phases please use the same device you did your group stage predictions on. Your device will remember who you are and your predictions so you can keep track of your results and to link all of your predictions to your original entry. If however you lose your predictions use the box below to restore them using your last saved code.

  • Key Dates:
  • Round of 32: Submit by 4:00 PM on 28th June.
  • Round of 16: Submit by 4:00 PM on 4th July.
  • Quarter-finals: Submit by 4:00 PM on 9th July.
  • Semi-finals: Submit by 4:00 PM on 14th July.
  • Final: Submit by 4:00 PM on 19th July.

🏆 Scoring System

  • 3 Points: Correct Match Result AND Correct Scoreline.
  • 1 Point: Correct Match Result only (Home win, Away win, or Draw).
  • 5 Points: Correct Bonus Answer (Golden Boot, Golden Glove, Player of the Tournament, or Young Player of the Tournament).
